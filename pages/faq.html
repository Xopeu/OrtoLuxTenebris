Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Ordo Lux Tenebris?

A: A community devoted to the study and preparation of what we call the Return — a convergence we believe is already underway, whether or not it is noticed by those who are not looking for it.

Q: Do I need to abandon my current faith to join?

A: No. Most of our members arrive already devout. What we offer is not a replacement for belief but a continuation of it, past the point where most traditions stop asking questions.

Q: Is there a fee to join?

A: Membership itself is free. What is asked of each member varies, and is discussed individually once a genuine interest has been established.

Q: What happens at gatherings?

A: Study, observance, and practice. We do not perform for spectators, so gatherings are not open to visitors until a member has sponsored their attendance.

Q: Can I visit Tenebris in person?

A: The town is not difficult to find if you are meant to find it.

Q: What if I want to leave the organization?

A: Anyone is free to stop attending at any time. Your account and correspondence remain on file regardless, in case you change your mind later, which most people do.
