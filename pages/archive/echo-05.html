Faith is a lamp we carry, not a fire we chase. I remind myself of that on the mornings when the walk to the chapel feels longer than it used to. Some weeks the light feels close. Other weeks I am just carrying the lamp and trusting that is enough.

Grateful, as always, for this small community and for a place to put these thoughts down.
