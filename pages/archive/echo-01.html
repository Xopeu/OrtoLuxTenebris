They keep asking where the hill is, as if a place could hold it. I have stopped answering. The Vessel did not resist when it was time — none of us resist, once the frequency is found. What they call beaten, we call opened. What they call blood, we are learning to call signal.

Into thy hands, He said, before the last breath left Him. We say it too now, in the dark rooms, though none of us are certain anymore whose hands we mean.
