There is a tower on the coast in my hometown, and as a child I believed its light was there to warn ships away from the rocks. I understand now that a light which calls a ship toward it and a light which warns a ship away look identical from a distance.

Ours does not warn. Ours calls. I did not understand the difference until I was already close enough to see what was waiting at the base of the tower.
